PRECISION ENGINEERING

Based in Norfolk, Area 31 Industries is a specialist automotive workshop dedicated to the art of vehicle perfection.

From brand-new RAMs to iconic Classic VWs, our fully secure facility is designed to provide every vehicle the space and attention it deserves.

We don't just fix cars; we elevate them. Most of our clients see their vehicles restored to better than factory spec.

Capabilities

Custom Modifications

Engine rebuilds and bespoke engineering. If the part you need doesn't exist, we'll manufacture it in our in-house machinery workshop.

Restoration

Full-scale restorations with a focus on longevity. We specialize in internals and undersides to keep your vehicle running smoothly for decades.

Performance Tuning

Upgrades for suspension, turbos, brakes, and exhausts. Specialized tuning for road, track, or show.
